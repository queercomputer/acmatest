Install the Scam Study app

Installing a web app is easy, but it might be new to you. That's okay! Here's a look at how you can install this app for most device types.

iOS (iPhone)

Look for the share button in Safari, select "Add to Home Screen," and then just follow the prompts.

Android

1. Click on the three dots at the top right of the screen and find the words "Add to Home screen"

2. This will open a box that says "Add to home screen". Click the "Install" button.

3. This will put an easy-to-use button for quick access on your phone screen. The button looks like this and is called “Scam Study”.

Remember that if you’re having any trouble with the button or installing, you can just go to the interface on a browser on your phone by clicking the link in your daily SMS reminder.
