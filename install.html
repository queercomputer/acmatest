Install me:

Installing a PWA is easy, but it might be new to you. That's okay! Here's a look at how you can install this app for most device types.

Back to index

iOS

Look for the share button in Safari, select "Add to Home Screen," and then just follow the prompts.

Android

Look for the "More" menu. This is the "three dots" menu in Chrome but could be different depending on your brpwser. Next, select "Add to Home Screen," and then just follow the prompts.

Desktop

Different browsers work differently, but look for an install icon in the URL bar of your browser, click, and follow the prompts.
